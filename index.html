About Me

I am a Robotics & AI Engineer working at the intersection of robot perception, soft robotics, and medical devices. My work spans MuJoCo/MJCF simulation, manipulation, RGB-D perception, embedded systems, and digital-health wearables. I enjoy building end-to-end systems that go from theory and simulation to real-world devices and robots.

MSc Robotics

MBZUAI, Abu Dhabi, UAE

Robotics Projects

  • Vision-driven Soft Manipulation on a Quadruped: SLAM-based navigation with a quadruped platform, RGB-D perception using RealSense, YOLO-based object detection, and GPT-4.1 Vision for high-level scene understanding; soft gripper used for manipulation.
  • 5-Finger Soft Robotic Hand in MuJoCo: Detailed MJCF model of a tendon-driven, five-finger hand with joint limits, actuators, and contact modelling, used for simulation of grasping and learning-based control.
  • Object Material Classification for Safer Grasping: Vision-language models and embedded sensing to classify object material (soft/medium/hard) and adapt grasp strategy for fragile vs rigid objects.
  • RGB-D Perception & Localization Pipeline: RealSense-based pipeline combining depth point clouds and RGB detections (YOLOv8) for 3D localization and mapping, integrated with ROS.
  • Robotic Hand Emulator: Wearable sensor system that maps human finger motion to a robotic hand in real time using flex sensors and microcontroller-based control.

Medical

Medical Devices & Digital Health

Work focused on medical devices, prosthetics, digital health, intraoral tracking, and intelligent wearables for monitoring and rehabilitation.

Healthcare Experience

Apr 2025 – Sept 2025

Research Intern – AI for Mandibular Tracking

Dubai Health, Dubai, UAE

Developed an AI-assisted magnetic intraoral system for 3-DoF mandibular position tracking. Built a large-scale dataset using a Dobot E6 robot and MMC5983MA magnetometer, achieving sub-millimetre accuracy for jaw tracking relevant to TMD, orthodontics, and sleep medicine.

2020 – 2024

B.Tech Capstone – Soft Prosthetic Hand

Sanjivani College of Engineering, India

Designed and developed an automatic silicon-moulded soft prosthetic hand and low-cost myoelectric arm for upper-limb amputees, using EMG sensing and embedded control to provide accessible assistive technology.

2023

Low-Cost Myoelectric Prosthetic Arm

IEEE ICCAKM (Publication)

Co-authored work on a low-cost myoelectric prosthetic arm that leverages EMG signals and embedded hardware to provide functional grasping for upper-limb amputees.

Medical & Wearables Projects

  • AI-Assisted Magnetic Intraoral Mandibular Tracking: Machine-learning-based 3-DoF mandibular tracking system using tri-axial magnetometer data and robotic ground truth, targeting applications in TMD, orthodontics, and sleep-related breathing disorders.
  • NutriSpecs – Smart Glasses for Food Intake & HR Monitoring: ESP32-S3-based smart glasses combining an OV2640 camera, MAX30102 heart-rate sensor, and YOLOv8-nano, enabling on-device food detection, caloric estimation, and fusion of HR with intake events.
  • Embedded Urban Snore Detection System for Early OSA Screening: Embedded classifier for snore vs non-snore detection on an ESP32-S3, with quantized models and strict resource constraints, aimed at early risk screening for obstructive sleep apnea.
  • Comparative Nasal Airflow Measurement: Dual-sensor nasal airflow measurement system with live streaming and synchronized logging to compare different respiratory sensing modalities across breathing tasks.
  • Brain Impact Simulator: STEM/MedTech project where a helmet-mounted system detects which brain lobe is most impacted during collisions, aiding triage and decision-making after accidents.
